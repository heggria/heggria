## What I care about

Software is never only implementation. It is also a record of assumptions, trade-offs, and responsibility.

I am interested in tools that make their boundaries visible, systems that can explain what happened, and work that remains understandable after the conversation ends. My public notes connect engineering practice with the ideas behind it.
